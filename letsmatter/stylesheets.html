Styling your webpage

What is styling?

Styling is when you change the visual aesthetic of your webpage. Visual aesthetics can be things like:

  • color of text
  • size of text
  • background color
  • spacing of lines
  • font
  • ...and much MORE

DIRECTIONS

As usual, download and open this page in your text editor. Read through the comments in the STYLE tag to learn about styling.
Then, in the style tag, do the following:

  • Change the BACKGROUND COLOR of the webpage to your favourite color.
  • Change the FONT of the ENTIRE page.
  • Change the COLOR of the paragraph text.
