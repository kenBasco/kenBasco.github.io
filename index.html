About

My name is Kenneth Basco. I am a spring 2019 graduate of Rutgers University. My undergraduate degree is a major in ITI (Information Technology and Informatics) with a specialization in Game Production and Innovation as well as a minor in Computer Science. I mediate the relationship between people and information technologies. This ranges from technical support to the prototyping and development of your next product. I’m interested in how information is represented, and consumed by its audience. Sometimes it’s a two-way street. My work includes but is not limited to games, zines, instructional materials, scripts, websites, product descriptions, user interfaces, meditative vignettes, and CAD drawings. I am a strong believer in work that creates itself, transcending deliverables that take new meaning and interpretation.
